NUMARK’S NS7III CONTROLLER NOW AVAILABLE 01:23:09 Today | News | Equipment

New Numark’s NS7III controller for Serato DJ is a premier four-deck with high-resolution full colour LCD screens, stackable waveform display and an interactive control surface.

There’s no other motorised controller on the market besides the NS7III allowing a real turntablist to have a true and authentic spinning experience. It makes DJ’s focus on their mixing, craft and performance.

You can enquire about this controller at these Viva Afrika stores: Carlton Centre, Johannesburg – +27 (0) 11 331 1592 Rosebank – +27 (0) 11 250 3281 Durban – +27 (0) 31 301 0474 Some of NS7III features:

Real-time visual feedback

The NS7III fuses Serato DJ’s music library with the desired response of a professional DJ turntable. It has a real-time visual feedback of Serato DJ via three vibrant, 4.3-inch full-colour LCD screens.

Two of the screens provide moving waveforms, playhead, deck, and FX status. The third central screen can be changed to display stacked parallel waveforms for complete beat matching control of your active programs.
